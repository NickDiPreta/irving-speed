How It Works

Breaking the chicken and egg cycle of building credit.

At Perch Credit our mission is to eliminate the barriers of credit building.

Why Perch?

Perch Credit was built out of necessity when our co-founder, Michael Broughton, was trying to figure out a way to fund his education after being denied a loan, due to a lack of a credit score. To get a credit score, he was told that he needed to have a credit history. But, credit history can only be achieved by receiving loans or credit cards. See the problem? Out of frustration and a great deal of research, Perch Credit was created.
